A #VizForSocialGood project, with Dear Tech People


Perimeter & methodology

  • They chose 100 tech companies, focusing on the "darlings" - the ones that repeatedly show up on top lists
  • analyzed thousands of profiles pulled from online sources, such as LinkedIn, and consolidated diversity numbers at a company level and for tech (Software Engineer, Engineering Manager, Analytics ...) and leadership roles (CEO, CTO, CMO ...)
  • created overall, technical and leadership rank to better compare companies

When we loose diversity on the way to tech and leadership teams, and the missing women of color

What we don't clearly see here :

  • The double penalty of not being white AND a women : what are the percentage of women of color in companies ?
  • Diversity in technical of leadership is usually less than overall : how the diversity change across positions ?


Let's introduce a new representation to better visualize those two information, with the average company data.

For each role and overall we compute the % of non-white people (asian, black, latinx, other) relative to the role, women, and women of color (non white wowen) to better see their representation evolution across those positions


To better visualize women of color and evolution across position, I did supress here the race details, refer to dear tech people website or data for more detail or race repartition
